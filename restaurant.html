Restaurant Menu UI Prototype

Type: UI Concept Design | Platform: Tablet/Desktop | Tool: Canva

Overview

This project is a modern restaurant menu UI prototype designed to provide a visually engaging and structured browsing experience. The goal was to simulate a digital restaurant interface that enhances readability while maintaining aesthetic appeal.

Problem

Many digital restaurant menus appear cluttered, poorly structured, and visually overwhelming. Customers often struggle to quickly navigate categories or locate items due to inconsistent layout and lack of visual hierarchy.

Objective

To design a clean, organized, and user-friendly digital menu interface that improves clarity, enhances visual structure, and allows users to browse menu sections effortlessly.

Solution

The prototype was structured using clear category divisions, balanced spacing, and consistent typography. Visual hierarchy was applied to highlight food categories and item descriptions. A cohesive color palette and layout alignment were used to create a modern and inviting digital menu experience.

Design Approach

The design process focused on:

• Clear category separation
• Consistent typography
• Balanced spacing and alignment
• Visual hierarchy for readability
• Aesthetic yet minimal color palette

Device Context

This interface was designed for tablet/desktop viewing to simulate a digital restaurant display or an online browsing experience.
